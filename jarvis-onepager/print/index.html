// the thesis

Jarvis exists because the durable moat against well-funded autonomous-execution agents is open knowledge infrastructure that closed AI vendors structurally cannot replicate. The bet is that a Markdown-native, vendor-neutral knowledge surface becomes the layer those agents query rather than rebuild. The window is now because the autonomous-agent market is racing to ship execution UX while leaving the knowledge substrate unclaimed.
